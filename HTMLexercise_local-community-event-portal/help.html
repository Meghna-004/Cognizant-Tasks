How to Use This Portal

  • Go to Events to browse community activities
  • Use Register to sign up for an event
  • Check the confirmation message after submitting your form
  • Use Find Nearby Events to locate events close to you

Common Issues

Form not submitting?
Make sure all required fields are filled in correctly.


Video not loading?
Wait for the "Video ready to play" message to appear before pressing play.


Preferences not saving?
Make sure your browser allows localStorage. Check Settings → Privacy.


Location not working?
Click "Allow" when your browser asks for location permission.
